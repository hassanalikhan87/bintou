# Bintou Brand Guidelines (Work in Progress)

## 1. Brand Identity
**Name Meaning:**
*   **Bintou**: Derived from "Binte" (daughter) and "Bijou" (jewel in French).
*   **Essence**: Embodies the spirit of a daughter of jewels, a mother of heritage. She is the keeper of stories, the hands that remember, and the heart that crafts.

**Tagline:**
*   "You don't wear our pieces. You inherit them."

**Brand Core:**
*   **"Old Souls, Modern Hearts"**
*   A celebration of legacy, love, and craftsmanship passed through generations.
*   Expressed through intricate, soulful silver jewelry designed for the modern spirit.

## 2. Brand Values
*   **Crafted with Care**: Meticulous attention to detail and intricate workmanship.
*   **Rooted in Legacy**: Inspired by heritage, handcrafted to honor tradition.
*   **Truth in Quality**: A promise of authenticity, durability, and finesse.
*   **Spiritual Simplicity**: Jewelry that speaks to the soul, not just the eye.
*   **Trust Built Slowly**: We believe in relationships, not just transactions.

## 3. Brand Voice
**Tone:**
*   **Warm. Poetic. Grounded.**
*   "Bintou speaks like a wise woman — graceful, soulful, never loud. Her words feel like heirlooms, passed down gently from one heart to another."

**Language:**
*   Metaphorical, heritage-inspired, sensory-rich.

**Pacing & Rhythm:**
*   **Cadence**: Calm and deliberate.
*   Avoid choppy sentences. Let the words breathe.
*   **Sentence Length**: Vary between short emotional punches and flowing poetic lines.

**Examples:**
*   ✅ "Our hands know the stories our hearts don't always tell."
*   ❌ "Crafted. Styled. Worn."

## 4. Brand Narrative
*   "She is a mother. A maker. A memory keeper."
*   "With hands trained by tradition and a heart tuned to today, she carves stories into silver."
*   "Each piece carries the essence of her heritage — a whisper of devotion, a symbol of resilience, and the quiet poetry of being a woman across generations."
*   "This is not just jewelry. This is generational wealth — redefined."
*   "Not in gold or property, but in skill, in spirit, and in the stories we choose to wear."

## 5. Vision, Mission & Purpose
**Vision:**
*   A world where jewelry is not just worn — it is remembered, inherited, and cherished as legacy.

**Mission:**
*   To craft soulful silver jewelry that carries meaning — blending traditional craftsmanship with modern elegance — and honoring the legacy of women through every intricate design.

**Purpose:**
*   To preserve the sacred art of silver-making and redefine generational wealth — not in material excess, but in skill, love, and the stories we choose to wear and pass on.

## 6. Color Palette
**Philosophy:**
*   Rooted in earth and emotion.
*   Hues that whisper legacy, memory, and grace.
*   Evokes warmth, depth, craftsmanship, and spiritual simplicity.

### 6.1 Primary Palette
*   **Heirloom Saffron (`#A9723E`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Warm, golden-brown. Evokes tradition (turmeric, spice boxes, vintage silk). Represents legacy, warmth, craftsmanship.
    *   *Symbolism*: Heritage passed down | Eternal warmth | Time-honored stories.
*   **Ivory Dust (`#F9F2E8`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Soft, understated. Like lace or fine dust on albums. Reflects purity, simplicity, spiritual clarity.
    *   *Symbolism*: Gentle femininity | Introspection | Soulful elegance.
*   **Sacred Garnet (`#813235`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Deep, bold, grounding. Mirrors strength of generations of women. Represents resilience, memory, emotional depth.
    *   *Symbolism*: Bloodline | Devotion | Generational strength.

### 6.2 Accent Palette
*   **Dust Rose (`#C9A6A0`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Muted pink, faded roses, pressed letters.
    *   *Symbolism*: Gentle nostalgia | Tender femininity | Intimacy.
*   **Jewel Smoke (`#B2B6B7`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Misty grey, old silver polished with care.
    *   *Symbolism*: Wisdom of age | Mist of memory | Calm assurance.
*   **Ash Plum (`#564B53`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Shadowy plum, twilight rituals.
    *   *Symbolism*: Inner knowing | Dusk prayers | Emotional depth.
*   **Indigo Prayer (`#2B3A4F`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Deep, meditative, star-strewn nights.
    *   *Symbolism*: Sacred rituals | Clarity of thought | Trust.
*   **Aqeeq Green (`#035851`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Bold, spiritual, healing.
    *   *Symbolism*: Divine connection | Healing energy | Sacred adornment.
*   **Shadowed Wine (`#74364D`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Deep, wine-kissed plum, sealed letters.
    *   *Symbolism*: Feminine mystery | Old-world elegance | Unspoken depth.
*   **Clay Ember (`#994A3A`)**:
    *   *Meaning*: Earthy, grounded, kiln-fired terracotta.
    *   *Symbolism*: Maker's hands | Warmth of earth | Passion in process.

### 6.3 Color Combinations
*   **Signature Look (Packaging & Identity):** Ivory Dust + Sacred Garnet + Heirloom Saffron. (Soft base, powerful accents).
*   **Emotional & Poetic (Print/Cards):** Dust Rose + Ivory Dust + Jewel Smoke. (Soft storytelling).
*   **Spiritual & Mystical (Features/Cards):** Indigo Prayer + Aqeeq Green + Ivory Dust. (Healing, divine energy).
*   **Product Display & UI:** Clay Ember + Shadowed Wine + Ivory Dust. (Grounded, dramatic).
*   **Craft & Heritage:** Heirloom Saffron + Ash Plum + Jewel Smoke. (Skill, process).

### 6.4 Usage Rules
*   **DO:**
    *   Use **Ivory Dust** as default background.
    *   Use **Heirloom Saffron** & **Sacred Garnet** as anchors (CTAs, Logos).
    *   Use **Jewel Smoke** & **Ash Plum** for typography in specific formats.
*   **DON'T:**
    *   Place **Sacred Garnet** next to **Shadowed Wine** or **Ash Plum** without separation.
    *   Overuse **Aqeeq Green** or **Indigo Prayer**.
    *   Use full-color backgrounds in **Clay Ember** or **Dust Rose** without balance.

## 8. Logo Guidelines
**Variations:**
*   **Primary Logo**: Arabic calligraphy + "B I N T O U" (Serif) + Tagline.
*   **Secondary Logo**: "B I N T O U" (Serif) + Icon.
*   **Logo Mark**: Icon only (Diamond/Spark shape).

**Color Usage:**
*   **Primary**: Heirloom Saffron, Ivory Dust, Sacred Garnet.
*   **Dark Backgrounds**: Use Ivory Dust.
*   **Prohibited**: Pure black on white, neon colors, bright primaries.

**Clear Space & Size:**
*   **Clear Space**: Height/width of two "nuqtas" (dots) on all sides.
*   **Minimum Size**: Print (25mm wide), Digital (100px wide).

**Incorrect Usage:**
*   Do not stretch, skew, or rotate.
*   Do not use unapproved colors.
*   Do not apply drop shadows or glows.
*   Do not add or remove elements.

## 9. Typography
*   **Primary Typeface (Headlines)**: **FreightDisp Pro** (Classic serif, elegant, timeless).

## 10. Patterns
